About us

We offer both IV ketamine and intranasal FDA approved, insurance covered nasal esketamine (Spravato.) Dr. Prashad understands the importance of creating unique treatment plans for each patient including combining ketamine with oral medications or Brainsway’s deep TMS in order to maximize results. In addition, each infusion is specifically tailored to each patient. There is no pre-determined set time limit or dosage; we customize each treatment. Dr. Prashad has extensive experience with using ketamine and is one of the most experienced ketamine psychiatrists in the country.
